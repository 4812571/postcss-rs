# Binary Layout

+----------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+
|   Type(3) + index (13)          |    parent(16)                   |
+----------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+
|   source.start.line(16)         |    source.start.column(16)      |
+----------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+
|                      source.start.offset(32)                      |
+----------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+
|   raw flag(8)  |    raw.after   |                                 |
+----------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+

- type:
  - 000: `root`
  - 001: `atrule`
  - 010: `rule`
  - 011: `decl`
  - 100: `comment`
- index: 1 - 8192
